Research Projects

NLP sentiment analysis based on twitter keyword searching

I am working on this to make it better. check out my github

Tensorflow, scikit-learn, keras are used in this text-classification and deep learning task. I use Twitter API to get the information of tweets text, time and location of specific keywords. I use a dataset of 100,000 words with feature to train a Word2Vec model, tokenize it into pad sequence, normalize with Label Encoder to train a sequential model to apply the sentiment analysis. The overall accuracy is 0.73, with the training loss and validation loss both get close to 0.27

“Guerilla Antitrust – A Thought from Taobao Dispute”

In this project I worked with Prof.Tan on the project on ”Guerilla Antitrust – A Thought from Taobao Dispute”. The idea is motivated by the Taobao dispute in the e-commerce market in China. Guerilla antitrust, different from traditional antitrust, involves the small market participants, instead of reg- ulators, regulate the monopoly and dominant firms. Under certain circumstances, guerilla antitrust works better than traditional antitrust. We build a model to identify the conditions under which it will succeed.

“Spenthrift and Tightwads - Not anymore under mobile payments”

This idea comes from the popularity of mobile payments in China. I noticed that the mobile payment would have a positive impact on the consumption. However, the effect is different for different people. I did an online survey to identify the heterogeneity regarding a specific characteristics – whether they are spenthrift and tightwads. And I showed that the difference between the consumption behavior when using the mobile payment than using cash or card.
